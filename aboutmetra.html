Why Metra On The Go?

Everyone needs transportation and most people would rather take public transportation to their work rather than drive to save time and money. The problem we all face is that we needed to buy our tickets for the train manually, wait in a long line with the possible end result of missing the train.

Our Goal is to save money, time and trees.
